**GeoMMBench** is the first **expert-level multimodal benchmark** for interdisciplinary, multi-sensor geoscience and remote-sensing scenarios. It exposes the lack of rigorous evaluation in this domain.

**GeoMMAgent** is the matching multi-agent architecture that decomposes complex geoscience queries into specialized expert agents, achieving strong performance on the benchmark.

> Co-First Author. **CVPR 2026 Highlight**.
