A research project with **SenseTime** (advised by Dr. Jiahao Wang) on **Multimodal Large Language Models** for joint audio-visual understanding.

I designed and optimized multimodal fusion modules to enhance:

- Cross-modal representation learning.
- Temporal synchronization between audio and visual streams.
- Robustness on complex semantic-temporal alignment benchmarks.
