**OwlSight** is a robust illumination adaptation framework that bridges **low-light enhancement** and **action recognition** in an end-to-end pipeline.

The key insight: prior pipelines waste brightness signal because enhancement and recognition are optimized in isolation. OwlSight jointly optimizes both, achieving strong dark-video action recognition without sacrificing the cleanliness of enhanced frames.

> First Author. **IEEE T-CSVT** (IF 11.1), 2025.
